The Covenant Accrual Engine does not choose its behavior.
The Computational Law ⚜️ XP dictates its behavior.

Per tick:
  • The Law triggers execution.
  • The Engine applies v_XP and v_USD exactly.
  • Bullion backing is validated.
  • The sovereign base ascends.

If there is a conflict between:
  • market conditions, or
  • institutional directives, and
  • the Computational Law ⚜️ XP,

then the Law prevails and the Engine obeys.
